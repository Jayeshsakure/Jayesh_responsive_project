ALL NEW TVS APACHE RTR-310

Racing DNA Runs

With the #Freestyler, the evolution form puritan to play is complete.The ALL NEW TVS APACHE RTR_310 keeps the essence of the performance of theTVS APACHE RR-310 but strips away everything else to give you the naked sport that's engineered for play. The power. the rifing dynamics, the tech, all geared to let you truly express yourself with performance.

Design-Philosophy

The RTR 310 embraces a streetfighter-style silhouette, characterized by a forward-biased mass, a high raked tail, and an overall aggressive stance TVS describes it as “freestyler” styling.In essence, the Apache RTR 310 is built around a “freestyler” ethos a bold, expressive motorcycle that blends race-bred performance, tech-rich features, and a street-ready attitude. Its meant to feel agile, look unforgiving, and deliver both on the urban commute and spirited riding.

KNUCKLE GUARDS

The hand/knuckle guards installed on the TVS Apache RTR 310 these serve as protective shields mounted around the handlebar levers.Designed to shield the riders hands from external elements such as wind, debris, stones, and light impacts these guards enhance rider comfort and safety, especially during high speed riding or in challenging weather.

ADAPTIVE CYBORG HEADLAMP

The adaptive Bi-LED “Cyborg” headlamp featured on the TVS Apache RTR 310, capturing its aggressive,streetfighter aesthetic and high-tech lighting architecture.The headlamp draws inspiration from a cyborg giving the Apache RTR 310 its distinct,futuristic,and menacing look Automatic brightness adjustment:The headlamp dynamically adjusts both the throw (beam distance) and intensity based on the bike's speed and surrounding ambient light levels.

TRANSPARENT CLUTCH COVER

The transparent (clear) clutch cover designed for the TVS Apache RR 310 which is essentially the same as the RTR 310's engine platform.It's crafted from durable, high-grade aluminum alloy coupled with a heat resistant, transparent panel typically tinted titan grey to withstand engine heat while revealing the clutch mechanism behind it.
